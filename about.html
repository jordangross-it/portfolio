About

Hello! I'm Jordan Gross, an Information Technology graduate and web developer based in Orlando, Florida. Welcome to my personal website!


I created this site to practice and showcase my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, aiming for a sleek, modern dark-themed design. It’s a work in progress that reflects my commitment to continuous learning and building practical projects.


During my time at the University of Central Florida, I gained a solid foundation in web development, databases, operating systems, and programming. I’ve also worked with technologies like Node.js, Git, and front-end frameworks, applying them to hands-on projects that combine creativity with functionality.


Some of my personal projects include: AquaFocus – a mock-up landing page for an ocean-themed timer app, designed to improve focus and relaxation. Galaxy Planner – a space-themed web app for organizing tasks in a to-do list, built as my first React project, focusing on responsive design and an engaging user experience.


I’m passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and enjoy creating projects that are both visually engaging and practically useful.
